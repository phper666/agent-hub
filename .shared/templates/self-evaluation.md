# Self-Evaluation Template

> 每个角色在任务完成后向 `docs/current/feedback/{role}-self-eval.md` 写入自评。
> 参考 → `roles/{role}/SKILL.md` 中的 `## Self-Evaluation` 小节。

```markdown
# {Role} Self-Evaluation — {Task} — {Date}

## What Worked Well
- [List rules/skills/experts that helped]

## What Could Improve
- [List rules that didn't apply well]
- [List expert knowledge gaps]
- [List workflow friction points]

## Suggestions
- [Concrete suggestions for role/SKILL.md improvement]
```
